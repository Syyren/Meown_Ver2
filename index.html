Essential Tips to Raising a Healthy Cat!

Make sure you feed them!

Brush their fur every day!

Give them lots of cuddles!

Play with them lots!

Be sure they have a clean litter box!

Keep their nails trimmed!

A little treat every so often!


Why Does my Cat Attack Me?

Cats are funny little guys. Sometimes they'll just attack you for fun! When they do so, you'll notice they aren't trying to actually hurt you. They're just being playful!

Sometimes they'll attack because they're scared of you though. Have you been kind to your cat?
